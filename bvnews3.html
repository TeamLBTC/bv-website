• Platform will conduct events to create ‘Active Community Mobilization’ through volunteering. This will create ‘Active Citizens’ who will develop deeper sense of moral responsibilities for civic issues.

• BV works with a core motto of ‘Amplifying Civic Responsibilities in Individuals’ and is achieving this through empowering partner organizations to work with active local volunteers together with BBMP at their own localities, resulting in acceleration towards ‘Swachcha Bengaluru’ target.

•Most of BV events are designed to support the major steps of beautification given by BBMP such as – ‘Street Cleaning’, ‘Street Greening’ and ‘Street Walkability’

BV is on the verge of creating a system which will build end-to-end solutions for many community problems. We are measuring the impact of these groups in terms of sustainability of solutions, sustainability of Community Group and continuity of volunteering.

BV intends to work with government and people to gain meaningful inputs to build a scalable model of end-to-end solution and implement it throughout Bengaluru to help our city regain its grandeur by making it a socially and environmentally better place to spend our lives.
