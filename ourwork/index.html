What to do if you test positive for COVID in Bengaluru?


  • • Avoid the 3Cs:
    • a. Closed spaces with poor ventilation.
    • b. Crowds (more than 2 people).
    • c. Close Contact.
  • • Follow the 3Ws:
    • a. Watch your distance (social distancing of at least six feet)
    • b. Wear a Mask.
    • c. Wash your hands.
  • • Some of the Common early symptoms are:
    • Cough, Fever, Shortness of Breath, Sore Throat, Congestion, Headache, Body Aches, Sudden Loss of Smell and Sense of Taste
  • • Stay home: If you develop symptoms, don’t panic. The important thing is to stay at home and isolate yourself.

  • • Consult a doctor: If you notice symptoms, talk to a doctor, preferably through tele-consultation.

  • • Getting Tested: Until the results of tests are out, you should stay isolated at home as much as possible.

  • • What if you test positive? If you test positive, first inform your District Surveillance Officer (DSO)/BBMP local ward officials, and call one of the following helplines
    • • 14410
    • • 104
    • • 080-1077
    • • 080-22967200
