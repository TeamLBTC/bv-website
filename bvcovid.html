Bengaluru Veeraru team hosted a internal discussion with the officials of BBMP & Bengaluru City Police to understand all the ground challenges. After the discussion, Bengaluru Veeraru team called out all the NGOs registered in the BV platform for an online meeting in the third week of March and discussed how everyone could join hands and fight the pandemic in Namma Bengaluru.

After the brainstorming discussion with all the NGOs, BV took forward these initiatives:

  • 1. Research & documentation of FAQs of COVID19
  • 2. Awareness in communities
  • 3. Making Bengaluru Accessible by:
    • a. Mapping Essential Stores (Grocery, Provision, Utility shops).
    • b. Mapping Needy (Real Time Monitoring).
    • c. Mapping Containment Area
    • d. Mapping DCP – South Division
    • e. Mapping DCP – Central Division
  • 4. Assistance in Police Stations:
    • a. Food & Ration Distribution.
    • b. Vehicle Monitoring.
    • c. Police Station Documentation Support.
    • d. Volunteer & Donor Management.
    • e. Containment Area Management.
  • 5. BBMP South Zone:
    • a. Food & Ration Supply Management.
    • b. Private Donor Management.
  • 6. Ration Supply Team:
    • a. Sourcing Ration Kits from Partners
    • b. Distribution to Needy through identified Partners
    • c. Distribution to Needy who reached out to BV
    • d. Distribution to Police Stations
  • 7. Kannada Translators Team
    • a. Converting Kannada documents to English.
  • 8. BV Tech Team:
    • a. BV Covid-19 Dashboard
    • b. BV Work Analytics
    • c. Jot Forms

• Bengaluru Veeraru team took part in a discussion with the officials of BBMP & Bengaluru City Police to understand all the ground challenges.

• After the discussion, Bengaluru Veeraru team called out all the NGOs registered in the BV platform for an online meeting in the third week of March and discussed how everyone could join hands and fight the pandemic in Namma Bengaluru.

• NGOs voluntarily took up tasks in which they had experience and in-depth knowledge.
• Today after 50+ days, the BV Team has developed:

  • 1. Covid19 Dashboard.
  • 2. 20+ Maps benefiting 26 Police Stations & 44 Wards of BBMP.
  • 3. 10+ NGOs & Partners.
  • 4. 300+ Volunteers.
  • 5. 4765 Kits distributed.
  • 6. 2 Lakhs+ Life’s Touched.

Special thanks to all the NGOs, & Volunteers for their wonderful contribution for a better tomorrow
